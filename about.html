Summer Gate ApartHotel

Discover Summer Gate ApartHotel, ideally situated steps away from Maestral Beach and just 200 meters from La Petite Beach in Sarandë. Our charming aparthotel offers air-conditioned rooms, each featuring a private bathroom and balcony with stunning sea views.

Indulge in modern comforts including flat-screen TVs, well-fitted kitchens, and plush bathrobes and slippers. With family rooms available, we cater to all travelers seeking a peaceful retreat by the sea.

Conveniently located 700 meters from Saranda City Beach, Summer Gate ApartHotel invites you to experience the beauty of Sarandë in comfort and style.
